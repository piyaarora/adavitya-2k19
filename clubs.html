CLUBS AND GALLERIES

Various clubs and galleries where students showcase their talent

ARTESO

With the idea of spreading colours and brightness, ARTESO organises art exhibition at DAVIET, Jalandhar each year. Artists show impressive creations ranging from sketching, poetry, sculpting and painting etc. in various textures and mix media. This platform provides a Canvas of Imagination to all the budding artists. It's the collective energy of every associated individual that keeps us inspired, motivated and consistent in organising this wonderful event.

PRAGYAN

The Quiz club of Daviet has been actively been participating in various Quizzes.The members of Pragyan have created a group of students whose vision is to acquire knowledge.We are conducting Mega Quiz at Adavitya for past 3 years.All under the guidance of Dr.P.S MANN

ALFAAZ

Alfaaz is a collection of proud hardworking book worms and shy writers who believe that a pen is indeed mightier than a sword. Alfaaz provides a door of opportunity knocking on everyone’s door without any boundaries of language. A dreamland of good literature and poetry. To sum up, “Dil ki Awaaz Ko zahir kare Alfaaz”

ASHWA

Ashwa is the western dance group of the institute. The group participate in different western dance competition held at various institutes. The group also organise inter-college dance competition at our institue at Adavitya (Techfest).
